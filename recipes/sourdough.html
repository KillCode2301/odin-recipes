Sourdough Bread Recipe

Description

Sourdough bread is a type of bread made by the fermentation of dough using naturally occurring lactobacilli and yeast.

Ingredients

  • 500g bread flour
  • 350g water
  • 100g sourdough starter
  • 10g salt

Steps

  1. Mix the flour and water, and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Add the sourdough starter and salt, and mix until well combined.
  3. Let the dough rise for 3-4 hours, folding every 30 minutes.
  4. Shape the dough and let it rise for another 2-3 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to 450°F (230°C).
  6. Bake the bread for 30-35 minutes, until golden brown.
  7. Let the bread cool before slicing.
